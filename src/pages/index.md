# Empower Your Digital World

## Instant Productivity

Experience instant productivity as Geez transforms your digital workspace from a blank slate into a fully functional, efficient environment. Say goodbye to tedious manual configurations and hello to seamless productivity.

## Portability at Its Best

Geez offers unparalleled portability. Whether you're using the latest M2 MacBook Air, a trusted ThinkPad running Linux, or Windows with WSL, Geez ensures that your digital environment, tools, and configurations are always with you. Work seamlessly from any location.

## No Learning Curve

Geez is designed with user-friendliness in mind. You won't need to delve into intricate scripting languages or spend hours poring over extensive manuals. By answering a few straightforward questions, you're ready to start. Geez provides opinionated starter configurations, offering flexibility to match your preferences.

## Unlimited Access

From programming toolsets to the latest social media applications, Geez has your digital toolkit covered. With an extensive collection of programs and packages, you can effortlessly integrate new tools and applications. Adding a line to your `.geez.yml` file and invoking `geez apply` is all it takes to expand your digital horizons.

## Your Way, Your Rules

Geez is adaptable. When you reach a point where you want more control and wish to manage your digital configurations manually, simply use the `geez eject` command. Geez gives you the flexibility to shape your digital world according to your unique needs.

# Powerful Behind the Scenes

Geez serves as a user-friendly gateway to the robust Nix and Nix Home Manager. The user-friendly `geez` command shields you from the underlying complexity. With a straightforward 10-line YAML file edit, Geez leverages the full capabilities of Nix to ensure your digital environment is finely tuned and ready for action.
